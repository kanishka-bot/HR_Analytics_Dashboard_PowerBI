# HR Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition, compensation, and workforce satisfaction using the IBM HR Analytics dataset. Includes KPI tracking, attrition drivers by department/role/tenure, pay equity analysis, and an employee-level deep dive with dynamic filtering.

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition & Performance (Kaggle) — 1,470 employee records, 35 attributes.

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Summary
- KPI cards: Attrition Rate, Avg Tenure, Avg Monthly Income, Avg Age
- Attrition Rate by Department, Job Role, and OverTime
- Employee gender distribution

### 2. Attrition Analysis
- Attrition Rate by Age Band and Gender
- Attrition Rate by Tenure Band
- Attrition Rate by Marital Status
- Attrition Rate by Distance From Home

### 3. Compensation & Satisfaction
- Income vs Satisfaction scatter plot by Attrition
- Avg Monthly Income by Gender (pay equity check)
- Avg Monthly Income by Job Role
- Work-Life Balance distribution

### 4. Employee Profile / Deep Dive
- Filterable employee-level table (Department, Job Role, Attrition)
- Data bars on Income and Tenure columns

## Tools Used
Power BI Desktop, DAX (calculated columns & measures)
